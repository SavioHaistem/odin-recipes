Homemade Hamburger

Description

This homemade hamburger is a mouthwatering delight. The juicy beef patty mixed with onions, garlic, and a blend of condiments creates a flavorful and savory experience. Served in a soft bun with fresh lettuce, tomato slices, and your choice of cheese, it's the ultimate comfort food. Customize your burger with toppings like pickles, bacon, or jalapenos for an extra kick. A side of crispy fries or a salad complements this delicious burger perfectly. It's a classic meal that will leave you completely satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (topside, chuck, or your preferred cut)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your preferred type)
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, ketchup, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste and thoroughly combine all the ingredients until you have a homogeneous mixture.
  3. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape them into hamburger patties of your desired size.
  4. In a hot grill or skillet, cook the hamburger patties for about 3 to 5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. If desired, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty and let it melt.
  6. Cut the hamburger buns in half and lightly toast them in an oven or toaster.
  7. Assemble the hamburger by placing lettuce leaves and tomato slices on the bottom half of the bun.
  8. Place the hamburger patty with melted cheese on top and add mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard according to your taste.
  9. Cover with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
  10. Pair the hamburger with french fries, salad, or your favorite side dish.
